Words for different types of diets

Omnitarians eat meat and plants and maybe dairy and maybe eggs

Vegetarians eat plants and maybe dairy and maybe eggs and not fish

Vegans eat plants and not meat and not dairy and not eggs

Pescatarians eat fish and plants and dairy and eggs

Lacto-vegetarians eat plants and dairy and not fish and not eggs

Ovo-vegetarians eat plants and eggs and not fish and not dairy

Lacto-ovo-vegetarians eat plants and dairy and eggs and not fish

Lacto-pescatarians eat fish and plants and dairy and not eggs

Ovo-pescatarians eat fish and plants and eggs and not dairy

Lacto-ovo-pescatarians eat fish and plants and dairy and eggs
